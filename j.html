About DTC Automated System

The Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) Automated Bus Scheduling and Route Management System is a state-of-the-art solution designed to optimize public transportation in Delhi.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and sustainable public transportation for the citizens of Delhi, leveraging cutting-edge technology and data-driven decision making.

Key Features

  • Real-time bus tracking
  • Automated scheduling
  • Route optimization
  • Passenger information system
  • Traffic prediction and analysis

Benefits

  • Reduced wait times for passengers
  • Optimized routes based on traffic patterns
  • Real-time updates for better journey planning
  • Improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions
  • Enhanced safety through better fleet management
